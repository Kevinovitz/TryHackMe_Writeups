### Spotting Phishing Emails

1.  Classify the 1st email, what's the flag?

    In the first mail we can see a fake invoice from Paypal (legit), it creates a sense of urgency, and it spoofed the TBFC domain.

    ![Email1](https://github.com/Kevinovitz/TryHackMe_Writeups/raw/main/spottingphishing-aoc2025-r2g4f6s8l0/Phishing_-_Phishmas_Greetings_Email1.png)

    ><details><summary>Click for answer</summary>THM{yougotnumber1-keep-it-going}</details>

2.  Classify the 2nd email. What's the flag?

    In the second email we can see there is an attachment. The emails appears to come from TBFC domain and is impersonating someone.

    ![Email2](https://github.com/Kevinovitz/TryHackMe_Writeups/raw/main/spottingphishing-aoc2025-r2g4f6s8l0/Phishing_-_Phishmas_Greetings_Email2.png)

    ><details><summary>Click for answer</summary>THM{nmumber2-was-not-tha-thard!}</details>

3.  Classify the 3rd email. What's the flag?

    In the third email, we notice a sense of urgency. It impersonates someone from the company and uses social engineering to convice an action.
    
    ![Email3](https://github.com/Kevinovitz/TryHackMe_Writeups/raw/main/spottingphishing-aoc2025-r2g4f6s8l0/Phishing_-_Phishmas_Greetings_Email3.png)

    ><details><summary>Click for answer</summary>THM{Impersonation-is-areal-thing-keepIt}</details>

4.  Classify the 4th email. What's the flag?

    The fourth email is impersonating someone from the company while using an external sender domain. It uses social engineering to convince activities.

    ![Email4](https://github.com/Kevinovitz/TryHackMe_Writeups/raw/main/spottingphishing-aoc2025-r2g4f6s8l0/Phishing_-_Phishmas_Greetings_Email4.png)

    ><details><summary>Click for answer</summary>THM{Get-back-SOC-mas!!}</details>

5.  Classify the 5th email. What's the flag?

    The fifth email is more focussed on generating leads for the specified company.

    ![Email5](https://github.com/Kevinovitz/TryHackMe_Writeups/raw/main/spottingphishing-aoc2025-r2g4f6s8l0/Phishing_-_Phishmas_Greetings_Email5.png)

    ><details><summary>Click for answer</summary>THM{It-was-just-a-sp4m!!}</details>

6.  Classify the 6th email. What's the flag?

    In the sixth email we can see they are trying to impersonate the company using punycoding. And they use social engineering to elicit a response.

    ![Email6](https://github.com/Kevinovitz/TryHackMe_Writeups/raw/main/spottingphishing-aoc2025-r2g4f6s8l0/Phishing_-_Phishmas_Greetings_Email6.png)
